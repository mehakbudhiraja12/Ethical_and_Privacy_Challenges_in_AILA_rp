📖 Project Description

This research project focuses on the ethical and privacy challenges associated with AI-based Learning Analytics in modern education systems. The study explores how Artificial Intelligence technologies collect, analyze, and process student data to improve learning experiences while also raising concerns related to privacy, security, fairness, and transparency.

The project examines various ethical issues and challenges involved in the use of AI in education and discusses possible solutions for responsible and secure implementation.

The project highlights:

Student data privacy and security
Ethical concerns in AI-driven education
Algorithmic bias and fairness
Transparency and accountability in AI systems
Consent and responsible data usage

🎯 Objectives

To study ethical issues in AI-based learning analytics
To analyze privacy concerns related to student data collection
To identify challenges such as bias, surveillance, and lack of transparency
To explore methods for secure and responsible AI implementation in education
To examine future advancements in ethical AI systems

📊 Key Findings

AI-based learning analytics improves personalized education and student performance
Excessive data collection may threaten student privacy
Algorithm bias can create unfair academic decisions
Lack of transparency reduces trust in AI systems
Strong data protection policies are necessary for ethical AI adoption
